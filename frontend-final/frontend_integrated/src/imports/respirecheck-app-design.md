Design a clean, modern mobile health application UI for an AI-powered respiratory monitoring app. The app helps users analyze respiratory patterns by recording background noise and cough or sustained vowel sounds, removing environmental noise, and combining audio analysis with health and environmental factors to produce a respiratory risk score.

The design should feel trustworthy, minimal, and medical-grade, similar to apps like Apple Health or modern digital health startups.

Create a mobile-first design (iPhone frame) with the following screens:

Screen 1 – Home / Landing

Include:

App name: RespireCheck

Tagline: “Personalized respiratory monitoring through clean audio analysis”

Short explanation: “Record background noise and a cough or voice sample to analyze respiratory patterns.”

Primary button: Start Check

Clean health-tech visual style

Screen 2 – Record Background Noise

Purpose: capture environmental noise for noise reduction.

Include:

Title: Step 1: Record Room Noise

Instruction text: “Record 3 seconds of background sound without speaking.”

Large microphone record button

Timer indicator

Buttons: Record / Stop / Continue

Screen 3 – Record Respiratory Audio

Purpose: record cough or sustained vowel.

Include:

Title: Step 2: Record Respiratory Sample

Instructions:

Option A: “Cough naturally 2–3 times”

Option B: “Say ‘ahhh’ steadily for 4–5 seconds”

Large microphone record button

Audio waveform visualization while recording

Buttons: Record / Stop / Re-record / Continue

Screen 4 – Health & Context Form

Collect contextual data.

Include fields:

Age

Sex

Smoking status (Yes/No)

Allergies (Yes/No)

Existing respiratory condition (Yes/No)

Optional location/weather toggle

Primary button: Analyze Sample

Screen 5 – Analysis Loading Screen

Include:

Animated loading indicator

Text: “Analyzing respiratory sample…”

Subtext showing steps:

Cleaning background noise

Extracting acoustic features

Combining environmental factors

Screen 6 – Results Dashboard

Show the result clearly.

Include:

Risk Score card (0–100 scale)

Status indicator: Low / Moderate / High

Short explanation of results

Section titled Contributing Factors

respiratory audio deviation

environmental conditions

health factors

Buttons:

Retake Test

Save Result

Design Style

Use:

clean white or light background

soft medical blues and teals

rounded cards and buttons

modern minimal typography

simple icons and waveform graphics

Design should feel professional, intuitive, and trustworthy, optimized for quick user interaction and clear health feedback.
